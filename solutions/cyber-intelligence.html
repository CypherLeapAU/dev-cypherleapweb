Cyber Threat Intelligence: Shield Your Business

Cyber threat intelligence refers to the data and information within your organization about past, current, and potential cyberattacks. It helps identify vulnerabilities and provides insights into the tactics and motives of malicious actors, enabling businesses to understand and anticipate threats effectively. By documenting and analyzing this intelligence, organizations can build robust defenses and prepare appropriate incident responses. This proactive approach minimizes risks and strengthens cybersecurity, ensuring the protection of critical assets and data.

Dark Web Monitoring

The dark web is a network offering anonymity through encrypted communication. Your organisation’s sensitive data, such as financial details and credentials, may surface there, exposing you to cybercriminals and potential financial, reputational, and personal harm.
CypherLeap’s dark web monitoring service tracks such information, alerting you if it’s detected to mitigate breach impacts and protect your business, employees, and clients. We combine manual processes with AI-driven scans to monitor platforms frequented by cybercriminals.

Threat Hunting 

Cyber threat hunting is the proactive process of identifying threats that may have bypassed your organisation's endpoint defences. Our threat hunting involves actively and systematically searching your systems with the assumption that a breach may already exist. This approach helps uncover potential vulnerabilities or malicious activity before a breach triggers an alarm, allowing for earlier detection and resolution. 

Threat Intelligence 

Cyber threat intelligence encompasses the collection of data and information within your organisation regarding past, present, and potential cyberattacks targeting your business. 
We develop a comprehensive and well-documented understanding of cyber threats, including the motives, tactics, and techniques employed by malicious actors. Such insights are critical in helping you build strong security defences and implement effective incident response strategies.
